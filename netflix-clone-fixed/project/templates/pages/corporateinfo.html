How to change your netflex plan

You can change your netflex plan at any time. To change your plan, follow the step-by-step instructions below based on your device type.

Changing your plan online

  1. Sign in to your Account page on a web browser.
  2. Under Plan Details, select Change Plan.
  3. Choose the desired plan, then select Continue or Update.
  4. Select Confirm Change or Confirm.

As a reminder, plan upgrades take effect immediately so you can enjoy the added features right away. Plan downgrades take effect on your next billing date.
