Top Investor Questions

How does netflex account for content costs?

We cash-flow and capitalize content costs when they are produced or acquired. We then amortize these costs over the estimated viewing window of the content, which typically occurs on an accelerated basis where the majority of viewing occurs within the first two years of release.

What is netflex's capital allocation strategy?

Our top priority is to reinvest in our core business to drive long-term revenue growth and margin expansion. Second, we aim to maintain a robust investment-grade balance sheet. Third, we return excess cash to stockholders through share repurchases.

How does foreign exchange (FX) risk impact revenues?

Because we operate internationally but report in USD, fluctuations in currencies impact our revenue numbers. We generally align our pricing with local market conditions over time to compensate for foreign exchange movements, though there can be temporary impacts on our operating margins.
